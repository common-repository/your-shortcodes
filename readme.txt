== Description ==
Check all shortcodes which you can use on your site.

== Installation ==
= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Your Shortcodes'
3. Activate Your Shortcodes from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =
1. Download Your Shortcodes.
2. Upload the 'your-shortcodes' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate Your Shortcodes from your Plugins page.

= After Installation =
1. Cick 'See Your Shortcodes' on Plugins Page.
2. ,Or Cick 'Settings > Your Shortcodes'.
